01 Context

Keeping Alumni Connected
Across 10,000 Miles.

ASU's Chinese alumni community spans 15 cities — from Beijing to Chengdu, Shanghai to Shenzhen. After graduation, most of them disappear into busy careers with no reason to stay in touch with the university. The newsletter was the one consistent touchpoint designed to change that.

Every month, A-Highlights landed in 3,000+ inboxes with a curated mix of campus milestones, faculty spotlights, alumni career wins, and community events. The editorial challenge wasn't finding content — it was making institutional news feel personal, and making personal stories feel worth sharing.

"Consistency is its own form of brand building. Sixteen issues in a row means sixteen months of showing up."

03 Editorial Approach

Four Pillars,
One Voice.

Every issue followed a consistent editorial architecture — four content categories that gave the newsletter structure without making it feel formulaic.

—

学院动态 · Campus News

University rankings, research breakthroughs, campus construction, and leadership updates — translated from English sources and reframed for alumni relevance.

—

校友高光 · Alumni Spotlight

Career milestones, entrepreneurial ventures, and community contributions from the ASU China alumni network — sourced through WeChat groups and direct outreach.

—

全景速递 · Event Recap

Coverage of ASU alumni gatherings, happy hours, and online events — turning community moments into shareable content that drives future attendance.

—

节令特辑 · Seasonal Edition

Festival-themed issues for Spring Festival, Mid-Autumn, Graduation Season, and National Day — connecting the ASU calendar to the Chinese cultural calendar.
