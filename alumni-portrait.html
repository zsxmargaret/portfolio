01 The Concept

One Quote.
One Face.
One Story.

A面人生 — literally "the A-side of life" — was built on a simple editorial premise: every alumnus has a sentence that defines how they see the world. The series finds that sentence and puts it on a poster.

The design challenge was scalability without repetition. Each subject came with a different photo, quote, school, and personality. The template had to feel both consistent enough to build brand recognition and flexible enough to let each person's story breathe. ASU's gold and maroon provided the structure. Everything else adapted.

"The best alumni content doesn't talk about the university. It talks about the person — and lets the university be the backdrop."

02 The Series

Five Portraits.
Five Sentences.

Each poster pairs a portrait photograph with the alumnus's defining quote — chosen through an interview process and edited for impact.
